Courtney Scott "Court" McGee

Born December 12, 1984, Courtney Scott "Court" McGee is an American mixed martial artist in the Welterweight division of the Ultimate Fighting Championship. He turned professional in 2007 and initially competed in local promotions in Utah.

McGee gained recognition by winning Spike TV's eleventh season of The Ultimate Fighter. He has overcome a history of heroin addiction and is now dedicated to spreading awareness through anti-drug campaigns. He launched the "Hope 361" project to support addicts on their path to recovery.
